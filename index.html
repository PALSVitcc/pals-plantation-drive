PLANTATION DRIVE

Tree plantation is not just something that should be done just out of interest instead, it is a necessity, the urgent need of the hour. Planting trees is especially important to protect our environment against air pollution and global warming. To end this we reach out to you, young volunteers are encouraged to take this responsibility of organizing tree plantation campaign in their hands. We invite you to actively engage for this great cause and contribute as much possible from your side. Remember every small step counts. Be part of a noble cause to save your very own Earth, always remember we have only got one home.
